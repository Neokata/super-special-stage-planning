Notes / assumptions
  • Max potential income = 450 driver-tickets sold at the current bundle mix + $2,200 kickoff parking + other revenue. That's the ceiling if every route is sold out.
  • Fill % scales route revenue linearly. Parking is always shown at max ($2,200) since the lots are fixed cap. Move the slider to model partial sellout.
  • Bundle mix defaults to 30% / 45% / 25% (1/2/3-route). Adjust in the "Adjust bundle mix" panel. Note: must sum to 100% — a warning shows if not.
  • Per-tier max cars at 100% fill = 30% / 45% / 25% of 450 = 135 / 202 / 112. Revenue per tier: $10,125 / $27,270 / $20,720. Total route max: $58,115.
  • Scenarios (Conservative / Expected / Best Case) only affect cost defaults — the fill % slider is the single source of truth for revenue modeling. Switch scenarios when you have different cost assumptions (e.g. cheaper venue in Conservative, full vendor spend in Best Case).
  • Break-even fill = total cost ÷ max potential route revenue. Above this fill, the event is net positive.
  • Numbers persist via localStorage on this browser only. Clearing site data wipes them — copy out to a sheet if you need a real backup.
