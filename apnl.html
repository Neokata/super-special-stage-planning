Notes / assumptions
  • Scenario seeding: defaults below are conservative starting points — set Expected once you have real driver counts, Best Case once you know venue capacity.
  • Route ticket pricing: $75 / $135 / $185 hardcoded as defaults per 02-pricing-tiers.md. Editable per-cell if you want to test discounts.
  • Parking caps: 20 in-park, 40 reserved front — these are physical caps, "Sold" cannot exceed them visually but the subtotal will not auto-cap (overselling will just show as revenue above the cap; that's your problem, not the tool's).
  • Walk-in spectators are free, so they contribute zero revenue — no row for them.
  • All-in custom tier: for drivers paying something not matching the 3-tier (e.g. comped entries). Set Price + Drivers + Routes per driver and it computes.
  • Reset clears the active scenario back to its defaults. Other scenarios are unaffected.
  • Numbers persist via localStorage on this browser only. Clearing site data wipes them — copy out to a sheet if you need a real backup.
