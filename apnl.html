Notes / assumptions
  • Schedule. Four uniform sessions: Fri 7:30 AM + 2:00 PM, Sat 7:30 AM + 2:00 PM. Each session runs 5 different physical routes, 2 packs of 15 cars per route, 1 lead + 1 sweep driver per pack.
  • Capacity. 150 cars/session × 4 sessions = 600 driver capacity. Per-slot price $75.
  • Bundle pricing. Multi-session bundles: 1 session = $75, 2 = $125, 3 = $175, 4 = $225 (essentially buy-3-get-1-free). The P&L models this with a single editable Bundle discount % applied to gross route revenue — tune the % as your bundle-mix forecast firms up. Default is 0%. At 30%, $45,000 × 0.30 = $13,500 discount, net $31,500 (matches the old 3-tier ceiling).
  • Pack model. 5 routes × 2 packs × 15 cars/session × 4 sessions = 600 driver capacity. Per pack: 2 staff (1 lead + 1 sweep). 5 routes × 2 packs × 2 staff = 20 staff/session. Same staff runs both AM and PM of their pack (one pay per staff per day) = 20 staff-pays/day × 2 days = 40 staff-pays weekend. Driver-pay default is $300/staff × 40 = $12,000.
  • Max potential income at 100% sellout (no bundle discount):
    • 4 sessions × 150 cars × $75 = $45,000 routes
    • Plus $2,200 max kickoff parking (in-park $50 × 20 + reserved $30 × 40)
    • Plus raffle (flat editable — open to everybody at $5/ticket), sponsorship, merch
  • Raffle. $5/ticket, sold to everybody (drivers, walk-ins, spectators). Prizes are donated by sponsors — there's no prize cost row. Revenue is a single flat editable amount, not tied to driver count.
  • Insurance. Three labeled policies:
    • Friday morning (route coverage): $500
    • Friday night kickoff (park event): $2,000
    • Saturday all-day (5 routes × 2 sessions): $1,000
    • Total: $3,500
  • Break-even KPI (top-right card) shows how many cars (of 600) the event needs at the current cost mix to net $0. It's structural — does not move with the fill sliders — but does respond to the bundle-discount % (because the effective per-car revenue changes). Red card means the required number exceeds 600 cars; green means already break-even.
  • Driver swag (t-shirts + windshield banners + stickers, $10 + $10 + $1 = $21/driver) auto-scales with the per-slot fill sliders. At 100% sellout: 600 × $21 = $12,600 max.
  • Auto-linked per-attendance costs: t-shirts ($10/driver), windshield banners ($10/driver), stickers ($1/driver), digital marketing ($2/driver). Each is qty × $/unit where qty auto-derives from the fill sliders. Staff T-shirts are qty × $/shirt where qty is the editable staff headcount (default 40, $13/shirt).
  • Removed cost lines: dinner send-off (drivers pay for own meal), radios/comms rental (team owns all radios), medical / first-aid (no medic), DocuSign (not pursued), raffle prizes (donated).
  • Numbers persist via localStorage on this browser only. Clearing site data wipes them — copy out to a sheet if you need a real backup. The Reset to defaults button clears saved state and restores all cells to the original placeholders.
